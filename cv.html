Research

My research interests center around the intersection between sport and the environment, specifically on the ability of sport to encourage pro-environmental behavior and action. I focus on identity-based theoretical approaches and message framing. Methodologically, I utilize primarily quantitative methods within a pragmatic paradigm, with a focus on Bayesian statistical approaches.
